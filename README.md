# Nexora Solutions — Sales & HR MIS Dashboard 2024

## Project Overview
A comprehensive Management Information System (MIS) dashboard built 
for Nexora Solutions Pvt. Ltd., covering Sales performance and HR 
analytics for the financial year 2024.

## Live Dashboard
[View on Google Sheets](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1Q7QkNlrzto5TklhqdTftzpwyXWSJvHypx_eoOLTzjAM/edit?usp=sharing)

## Key Features
- 120-row transactional sales dataset across 4 regions & 3 products
- HR analytics: headcount, attrition rate, grade-wise distribution
- 15+ Excel/Sheets formulas: SUMIFS, COUNTIFS, VLOOKUP, IFERROR, TEXT
- KPI cards with conditional formatting (traffic light indicators)
- 4 interactive charts: Monthly trend, Region split, Product mix, QoQ
- Variance analysis: Actual vs Target, MoM growth, 3-Year CAGR

## KPIs Tracked
### Sales
- Total Revenue | Achievement % | Win Rate | Avg Deal Size
- Region-wise Revenue | Product-wise Revenue | Monthly Trend | CAGR

### HR
- Active Headcount | Attrition Rate | New Joiners | Dept Strength
- Grade-wise Distribution | Average CTC | Total Payroll
